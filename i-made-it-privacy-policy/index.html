Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Overview

We care deeply about your privacy. "I made it!" is built with privacy as a core principle. We don't maintain our own servers to store your personal data. Instead, all your data is synced directly to your iCloud account under your own control. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

2. Information Collection

Habit and Mood Tracking Data: When you create habits and log your daily mood, this information is stored on your iCloud account automatically. We do not have access to, store, or process this data on our servers. You maintain complete control and ownership of all your personal data.

App Performance Data: We may collect anonymized usage statistics and crash reports to improve app stability and performance. This information cannot identify you personally and helps us fix bugs and enhance features.

Apple ID: iCloud synchronization requires your Apple ID. We do not receive, store, or have access to your Apple ID credentials or iCloud account details.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use anonymized app usage data solely to:

  • Improve app stability and performance
  • Fix bugs and technical issues
  • Understand feature usage patterns to enhance the app
  • Ensure compliance with Apple's guidelines

We do not use your personal data (habits, mood logs, or any content you create) for any commercial purposes, targeted advertising, or third-party sharing.

4. Data Storage & Security

iCloud Storage: All your personal data is stored exclusively on Apple's iCloud servers, which are protected by industry-standard encryption. Your data is encrypted both in transit and at rest.

No Third-Party Servers: We do not maintain separate servers for your personal data. There are no additional intermediaries handling your habits and mood information.

Local Device Encryption: The "I made it!" app also stores a local copy of your data on your device, encrypted with iOS's built-in security features.

5. Third-Party Services

Apple & iCloud: We use Apple's CloudKit framework for iCloud synchronization. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy at apple.com/privacy to understand how Apple handles your data.

We do not share, sell, or disclose your personal data to any other third parties.

6. Your Rights & Control

Access & Export: You can access all your data within the app. To export or delete your data, you can manage your iCloud settings through your Apple device.

Delete Your Data: You can delete individual entries within the app, or remove all app data by uninstalling the app or managing your iCloud settings.

Opt-Out: You can disable iCloud synchronization in your device's iCloud settings, and the app will function with local storage only.

7. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy as our app evolves. Significant changes will be communicated through app updates or in-app notifications. Your continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
